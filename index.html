About Me

Hi, I'm Hyuntae Jeon, a senior CAE engineer at Renault Korea Central Research Center, working in the DE-TD CAE Team.

I have over 17 years of experience specializing in structural strength and durability analysis using ABAQUS and DesignLife.

My main expertise includes:

  • Global durability analysis of complete vehicle BIW (Body-In-White) structure
  • Fatigue damage prediction and evaluation
  • Strength analysis for Towing, Jack-up, Panel Stiffness, and Canning tests

Projects

  • Global Durability CAE: Simulating and evaluating full vehicle fatigue performance based on load cases.
  • Towing & Jack-up Analysis: Ensuring structural safety during towing and maintenance operations.
  • Panel Stiffness and Canning: Analyzing panel behavior under local stiffness and vibration conditions.
  • Fatigue Damage Evaluation: Predicting fatigue life using CAE methods to support vehicle durability design.
